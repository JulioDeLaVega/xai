The objective of this website is to provide a technical perspective on the compliance requirements regarding artificial intelligence.

It addresses topics such as discrimination, protection of personal data and bias in artificial intelligence.

The Gitbook proposes compliance documentation with the latest legislative and technical texts applicable to AI, focused on a technical approach.

The Google Colab puts regulatory requirements in practice in the planning, training and testing of an AI model.

If you wish to contribute or discuss a topic, you can contact me on Linkedin.
